Tense

PAST								PRESENT                        FUTURE
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 : Definite
2 : Contineous
3 : Perfect

*  S + V + O

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1 : Present Indefinite ( simple ):
	* work that we do again and again
	S + V (e/es) + O   ----> Singular    ( I is an Exception)
	S + V + O   ----> Plural

	I teach English.
	I teaches English  --> Wrong

	He, She, it   -> es/s
	They  -> only verb

	She sings a song
	They go to the Market
	Mira cooks food
	Ram and Rohan dance


	* In the case of negative

	S + (Helping verb) + not + V1 + O
	H.V => do / does
	if S == Singular ---> does
	if S == Plural  ----> do

	* when we use does/do then we dont use s in verb

	She does not sing a song
	They do not go the Market
	Mira does not cook food.

	* In Introgative form

	H.V + S + v1 + O

	Does she sing a song
	do they go to the market


2 : Past Indefinite (simple Past):
	* in past we repeat

	S + v2 + O

	* for positive -> v2
	* for interogative -> v1
	* subject not matter
	
	I ate food.
	You learned english
	They went to school
	I read story


	* for Negative
	S + H.V + not + v1 + O

	I did not eat food
	You did not learn english
	They did not go to school
	I did not read story

	* interogative

	Did + S + v1 + O

	Did I eat food
	Did you learn english
	Did they go to school
	Did I read story

	* Second type of Interogative sentence
	Q.W + H.V + S + v1 + O

	Why did I teach English

	* for the same negative
	Q.W + H.V + S + not + v1 + O

	Why did I not teach english

	* and with contraction

	Why didn't I teach english


3 : Future Indefinite ( simple future)

	S + will/shall + v1 + O
	I, we ==> shall, will

	I will teach english

	She will sing a song -> she will not sing a song -> will she sing a song



4 : Present contineous Tense:
	s + is/am/are + (v1 + ing) + o

	You are learning english.
	Mira is going to school.

	mira is not going to market

	is mira going to school.
	are you learning english

	what are you learning.


5 : Past Contineous (Progressive)
	
	was/were

	I was going to the market.
	she was eating food.
	they were watching TV


6 : Future Contineous

	will be

	I will be sleeping.
	Mira will be studying.
	you will be doing homework


	you will not be doing homework
	i will not be sleeping


7 : Perfect Present
	
	have/has

	S + has.have + v3 + o

	I have eaten food.
	you have studied.
	they have arrived.
	she has sung a song.


	i have not eaten food.
	you have not studied.

	have I eaten food.


8 : Past Perfect
	
	S + had + v3 + O

	I had eaten the food

	He had done the Homework

	They had talked


	I had not eaten the food
	He had not done the homework


	Had I eaten food


9 : Future Perfect

	S + will/shall + have + V3 + O

	She will have watched the TV

	I shall have slept

	They will have played the Cricket


	she will not have watched the TV
	will she have watched the TV


10 : Present Perfect Contineous

	S + have/has + been + V1 + ing + O +Since/For + Time

	I have been writing this book since january 2018

	She has been waiting for 2 hours
	She has not been waiting for 2 hours
	has she been waiting for two hours
	has she not been wainting for 2 hours


11 : Past Perfect

	S + had + been + V1+ing + O + since/for + time

	He had been waitinig for you  since 5'O clock

	I had been teaching english for 2 years


	He had not been .........
	had he been waiting for you.......
	had he not been .........


12 : Futute Perfect Contineous

	S + will/shall + have been + v1-ing + O + Since/for +  time

	She will have been watching TV since 8'O clock

	I shall have been cooking breakfast for half an hour


	she will not have been .....
	will she have been .....
	will she not have been.......
